Meet the team!

About us: We are one of the two Spring/Summer junior workshop sections for 2022/2023 at Drexel University! Our class is designed for us to make a vertical slice during the spring term and a completed game during the summer term! Our team is made of 12 students, who study Game Design Students or Computer Science, and our amazing professor! The game we are making is Left Hook. It is a 2D pixel art fishing/boxing game.
